Try Self-Retrieval First

Quick Self-Service Options

  • Dial *346# on the phone number you used during NIN enrolment — your NIN will be sent by SMS.
  • Send a text with your full name and date of birth to 346 (works on MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile).
  • Log in to the NIMC self-service portal if you have your enrolment details.

If any of the above methods work, you're done! If not — your phone number may have changed, or there may be an issue with your record — come to us and we'll resolve it.

When to Come to Us

  • → You've changed phone numbers since your original enrolment
  • → The *346# USSD code returned an error or no response
  • → You enrolled as a child and need your record migrated to adult status
  • → You enrolled a long time ago and the SMS was never received
  • → You need a printed NIN slip or NIN PVC card — not just the number
  • → You need a certified copy of your NIN slip for official use

Documents to Bring

  • ✓ Nigerian International Passport (valid or expired)
  • ✓ Nigerian Birth Certificate (NPC-issued)
  • ✓ Transaction ID from your original enrolment (if available)
  • ✓ Any government-issued ID (for secondary verification)

⚠️ Note

  • If your Transaction ID is unavailable, bring as many identity documents as possible — we'll retrieve your record using biometric matching.
  • Contact us before your appointment if you're unsure what to bring.

Retrieval Process

  1. Contact us

    Call or WhatsApp +447450 444858. Tell us whether you have your Transaction ID or NIN number so we can prepare the right approach.

  2. Book your appointment

    Retrieval slots typically take 20–30 minutes. We'll confirm the documents to bring based on your situation.

  3. Visit the centre

    Our officer verifies your identity and locates your record in the NIMC system using your biometric data and documents.

  4. Slip issued

    Once located, your NIN number is confirmed and a replacement slip is printed or arranged. If a physical NIN PVC card is needed, processing takes approximately 10 working days.
