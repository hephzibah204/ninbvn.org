How BVN Partnerships Work

The Bank Verification Number (BVN) is mandatory for operating any bank account, investment profile, or mobile money wallet in Nigeria. Because physical biometric capture is mandatory, diaspora centers and local offices can operate as capture points. Partnering with us grants you access to authorized capturing interfaces and secure database transmission portals.

Becoming a BVN Agent in the UK (Diaspora Captures)

A huge segment of the diaspora requires BVN registrations or links to open accounts, secure mortgages, or send money back home. We help you set up and configure bank-compliant capturing setups in major UK hubs.

Becoming a BVN Agent in Nigeria

In Nigeria, telecom shops, microfinance branches, and cyber cafes can offer BVN captures under our partner network. We guide your team through security parameters and software logins.

Hardware & Portal Requirements

To run active BVN captures, you must secure the following setup:

  • ✓ Capture Device: Secure Windows 10/11 Laptop with 8GB RAM minimum.
  • ✓ Biometrics: NIBSS/NIMC-approved ten-print or single-finger scanner.
  • ✓ Camera: HD camera with white background screen.
  • ✓ Workspace: A secure physical space with high-speed internet and power backup.
