How NIN Enrolment Partnerships Work

NIMC licenses large organizations to run diaspora and local biometric captures. Smaller companies, hubs, cyber cafes, and logistics centers can become sub-agents. Partnering under our accredited license grants you access to the official NIMC database capture software, allowing you to capture biometrics and process modifications legitimately.

Becoming an Agent in the UK (Diaspora Enrolment)

Operating a diaspora capture station in the UK is a premium opportunity. There are over a million Nigerians residing in the UK, many of whom require new NIN numbers or record modifications for passport renewal. We offer full technical support to set up certified capturing centers in key UK cities (London, Manchester, Birmingham, Glasgow, etc.).

UK Agent Support Focus

  • Provision of NIMC-certified software licenses.
  • Guidance on procuring and calibrating diaspora-grade biometric hardware.
  • Direct WhatsApp channel for troubleshooting and client database queries.

Becoming an Agent in Nigeria

In Nigeria, the demand for local capture kiosks remains extremely high due to mandatory links with bank accounts, voter registrations, and exam registrations (JAMB/WAEC). We assist local businesses in setting up sub-agent stations under our authorized vendor network, ensuring proper setup and training.

Nigeria Agent Support Focus

  • Local office location pre-screening to meet NIMC safety rules.
  • Assistance with system set up and operator registrations.
  • Access to local hardware suppliers for authorized fingerprint scanners and cameras.

Hardware & Office Requirements

To access the secure NIMC database portal, you must secure the following minimum requirements:

  • ✓ Capture Device: Windows 10/11 Laptop with 8GB RAM minimum and high security.
  • ✓ Biometrics: NIMC-approved single-finger or ten-print scanner.
  • ✓ Photo Capture: HD Webcam or digital camera with a plain white backdrop.
  • ✓ Office Space: A clean, accessible workspace with reliable internet and power backup.

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. Initial Contact

    Message our UK or Nigerian team via WhatsApp. We will evaluate your business structure and location suitability.

  2. Documentation Submission

    Submit your business registrations, ID copies, and photos of your workspace layout for vetting.

  3. Hardware Provisioning

    Procure the required biometric equipment. We will run remote tests to verify it meets NIMC specifications.

  4. Software Setup & Access Code

    Our administrators configure your secure VPN and install the official portal client. A unique tracking agent code is issued to your desk.
