Where can I register for Nigerian NIN in UK?

If you are a Nigerian citizen living in the UK, getting your National Identification Number (NIN) is mandatory for passport renewals, BVN updates, SIM registration, and other legal transactions. If you are searching for where can I register for Nigerian NIN in UK, you have several certified options. You do not need to fly back to Nigeria.

The National Identity Management Commission (NIMC) operates a network of licensed private enrolment partners across the United Kingdom. This framework, known as Nimc diaspora registration uk, allows secure biometric capturing at accredited local walk-in centres.

How to apply for a Nigerian NIN in the UK?

Wondering **how to apply for a Nigerian NIN in the UK**? The process is straightforward but requires physical presence for biometric collection:

  1. Fill out the NIMC Pre-Enrolment Form: Access the pre-enrolment forms online, fill out your demographic details, and download the printed summary sheet containing your barcode.
  2. Book an Appointment: Visit NINBVN.org's online scheduler to secure a capturing slot at a centre near you.
  3. Attend Capturing: Bring your printed pre-enrolment form along with your **valid or expired Nigerian International Passport** or an NPC-issued Birth Certificate.
  4. Biometric Data Capture: An enrollment officer will capture your fingerprints, facial photo, and signature.
  5. NIN Issuance: Your Transaction ID slip is issued immediately. NIMC processes the details and issues your official NIN number within 1–5 working days.
